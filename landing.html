SNS

①WeChat（微信）
It is like a Chinese version of LINE. You can't use LINE in China. It is very useful for shopping, searching for train transfers and so on.
②Weibo（微博）
It's an app like a Chinese version of Twitter. It allows you to write short blogs and many celebrities use it.
③Baidu(百度）
It's like a Chinese version of Google. You can search for anything with it. (Some words are banned by the government)
④Tiktok（抖音）
The Chinese version of TIKTOK, an app that allows you to watch a variety of short videos.
➄RED（小红书）
The Chinese version of Instagram is a great way to keep up to date with the latest Chinese trends.
