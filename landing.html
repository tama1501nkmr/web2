Sightseeing spot/旅游胜地

I have put together a list of the most famous tourist attractions.
①Tiananmen Gate (Beijing)/天安門/天安门
A huge gate with a portrait of Mao Zedong in the centre, red walls and a bright orange roof.
②INDIAN (Beijing)/頤和園/颐和园
This is the largest imperial garden in Beijing, built in the 18th century by the Qing emperor to celebrate his mother's longevity. It is a World Heritage Site.
③The Great Wall of China/万里の長城/长城
With a total length of over 20,000 km, it is the world's longest structure and a World Heritage Site, and one of China's most famous tourist attractions.

SNS/社交网络

①WeChat（微信）
It is like a Chinese version of LINE. You can't use LINE in China. It is very useful for shopping, searching for train transfers and so on.
②Weibo（微博）
It's an app like a Chinese version of Twitter. It allows you to write short blogs and many celebrities use it.
③Baidu(百度）
It's like a Chinese version of Google. You can search for anything with it. (Some words are banned by the government)
④Tiktok（抖音）
The Chinese version of TIKTOK, an app that allows you to watch a variety of short videos.
➄RED（小红书）
The Chinese version of Instagram is a great way to keep up to date with the latest Chinese trends.
